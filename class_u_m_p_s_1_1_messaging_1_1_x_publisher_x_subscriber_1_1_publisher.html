Detailed Description

A ZeroMQ publisher.

This is the publisher in a XPUB/XSUB socket. Here, we assume the proxy socket is the stable point of our network so with this publisher you connect to a socket whereas with the publisher in the traditional pub/sub you bind to a socke and allow subscribers to connect to you.
